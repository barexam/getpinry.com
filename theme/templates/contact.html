Before you contact us!

If you have a feature request or a bug, see below and please direct that to the proper location which is our Issue tracker, you can post about something quickly an anonymously. If it is a bug please be sure you include enough information for us to diagnose the issue.

Please do not spam us with your startup or open source ideas, we more then likely aren't interested in them. Head hunter services will automatically be dropped in our spam folders or hung up on if you call us.

That all being said we are always happy to hear from people interested in Pinry and wanting to see it grow so contact away!

Contact Pinry Devs

The quickest and easiest way to get in touch with us is via email at devs@getpinry.com. However, you can contact our active contributors by visiting their websites, we have many ways to get in touch with us from there.

Found a bug or want a feature?

Add it to our issue tracker on BitBucket! We double check and fix all bugs and are happy to read about your ideas for Pinry. However, please do a quick search through the issues to make sure it hasn't already been added.

BitBucket Issue Tracker

Want to be a contributor?

The best way is to clone our BitBucket repo, build out a feature or fix some bugs in a clean style that matches our own and if we like it we'll merge it! If you continuously provide Pinry with good updates we'll contact you or you can contact us with your track record and we can discuss getting you commit privileges.
